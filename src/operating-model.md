  <h1 id="operating-model-heading" class="page-title">Operating Model</h1>
  <p class="page-intro">Execution principles that align strategy, delivery, and governance at scale.</p>
</section>

<section class="section-block" aria-label="Operating model points">
  <ul class="content-list">
    <li>Align strategy, product, architecture, and risk into one planning cadence with explicit decision rights.</li>
    <li>Translate enterprise goals into measurable capability outcomes, then sequence delivery against clear dependency maps.</li>
    <li>Drive cross-functional execution through small governance loops that unblock teams without adding procedural drag.</li>
    <li>Build resilient operating mechanisms: service ownership, incident learning, and transparent reliability objectives.</li>
    <li>Develop leadership benches by pairing accountability with context-rich coaching across product and engineering.</li>
  </ul>
</section>
