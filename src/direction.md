  <h1 id="direction-heading" class="page-title">Platform Direction</h1>
  <p class="page-intro">Architectural direction that increases reuse, observability, and long-term system leverage.</p>
</section>

<section class="section-block" aria-label="Platform direction points">
  <ul class="content-list">
    <li>Productize core platform capabilities so domain teams can move faster with fewer bespoke integrations.</li>
    <li>Treat data contracts and interoperability standards as first-class platform products.</li>
    <li>Invest in observability, policy automation, and operational telemetry to enable proactive risk management.</li>
    <li>Balance innovation with simplification by retiring low-leverage complexity and reinforcing reusable foundations.</li>
  </ul>
</section>
